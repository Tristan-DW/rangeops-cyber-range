# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus on technical outcomes, not personal attacks.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Trolling, insults, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this policy and may restrict future participation when necessary.
